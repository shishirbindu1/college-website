A very warm greetings and welcome to the Pokhara University community! Since its inception in 1997, the University has shown a commitment to innovation and to serving the needs of its community and nation with a vision “to be a leader in the promotion of education through quality higher education and community service”. Within the short span of its existence, the University has achieved remarkable success and continues to forge its future with new program initiatives: a modern curriculum, research endeavors, student engagement, professional training, faculty development, international collaborations and much more.
